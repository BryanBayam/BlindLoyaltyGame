# Blind Loyalty

## Cutscene

### Scene 1
**Commander:** You’ve got a new mission from the president!

**Reuben:** What mission, Commander?

**Commander:** As usual, you need to eliminate a monster for the government.

**Reuben:** Could you give me more details about that?

**Commander:** For the past two years, the Bandit Village tribes have stolen our army’s food supplies. But they move like shadows. No one can ever catch them.

**Reuben:** So what’s your plan, Commander?

**Commander:** You need to find their secret bunker in order to prove that they stole the military supplies. Then we will launch massive troops to capture them, and the media will broadcast it to the world. Do you understand your task?

**Choice:**
- Yes, sir!

---

### Scene 2
**Narrator:** Before the world called you a hero, you were just a child who believed in order.

**Narrator:** Your name is Reuben. You grew up in a city that was constantly afraid. News reports spoke about threats, attacks, and instability.

---

### Scene 3
**Narrator:** Your parents used to say that the only reason people could sleep peacefully at night was because someone, somewhere, was willing to make difficult decisions.

**Narrator:** And that idea stayed with you.

---

### Scene 4
**Narrator:** You were not the strongest student, but you were disciplined. Focused. You believed rules existed for a reason. While others questioned authority, you believed in protecting people, even if it meant doing things that others would not understand.

---

### Scene 5
**Narrator:** At the age of nineteen, you were recruited. The Special Force saw potential in you: calm under pressure, loyal, and capable of making decisions without hesitation.

---

### Scene 6
**Narrator:** Over the years, you became one of their trusted operatives. The missions were clean, efficient, and necessary.

**Narrator:** At least, that’s what you were told.

## Game 01 — Find the Bunker of the Bandit Village

### Scene 7
**Reuben:** This does not look like a bandit village.

**Reuben:** Something weird is going on.

**Reuben:** Forget it. I have to focus on the mission.

**Reuben:** I finally found the bunker location.

**Boss Bandit:** Wait! Why are you here? Why is the government sending soldiers here?

**Reuben:** Don’t act innocent. You bandits are stealing our nation’s supplies!

**Boss Bandit:** What? No, that’s not true. Why would we—

**Reuben:** Shut up! Put your hands where I can see them!

**Boss Bandit:** Believe us. We are good citizens. But my sister betrayed us, joined the government, and helped them steal our land and food supplies. Just look around.

---

### Scene 8
**Soldier:** Move! Work faster!

**Soldier:** You think food grows by itself? Keep going!

**Narrator:** Reuben witnesses farmers crying, soldiers forcing them to work, and children starving.

---

### Scene 9
**Reuben:** Wait... can you explain what you mean?

**Commander:** That’s enough.

**Commander:** Don’t fall for it, Reuben. Arrest her!

---

### Scene 10
**Narrator:** The Boss Bandit is handcuffed by two Special Force soldiers.

**Commander:** Good job, Reuben. We’ll take it from here.

---

### Scene 11
**Commander:** Wake up. You got a new mission!

**Commander:** This is a very serious situation. Our intelligence has informed us that Oasis Media is planning to broadcast news claiming that the government is attacking villagers to seize their land.

**Reuben:** No way. When will the broadcast start?

**Commander:** Tomorrow. So you need to go quickly.

**Choice:**
- I’ll try my best!
- Yes, sir!

**Commander:** I’ll be waiting at the President’s Office. Make sure to contact me after you come back.

**Commander:** Oh, if you see any classified documents, don’t open them.

## Game 02 — Find the Secret Document of Oasis Media

### Scene 12
**Reuben:** I need to be stealthy to attack him from the back.

**Reuben:** Finally, I stopped you from sharing the news. That was such a pain to do.

**Reuben:** Now where is that document?

**Reuben:** Finally, I got the document. I need to send it to the Commander right away.

**Oasis Media CEO:** Wait... I know you are a good person. Trust me. Please don’t send it to the government.

**Reuben:** But why?

**Oasis Media CEO:** You can open the document and judge it by yourself.

**Reuben:** But the Commander said I should not open this document.

**Choice:**
- Open the document
- Close the document

## Route 2 — Close the Document

### Scene 14.2
**Commander:** Good job once again, Reuben! You are doing a perfect job.

**Commander:** Oh, and also, the President wants to meet you.

**Reuben:** Alright, Commander.

**President:** Are you Reuben, the member of our Special Force?

**Reuben:** Yes, sir.

**President:** Brilliant! You execute every task perfectly. Now I’ll give you one final task.

**Reuben:** What is that, sir?

**President:** You need to attack the Ashat Tribe tomorrow before the sun rises. They want to rebel against the government, so we need to strike first before they come for us.

**President:** I will also give you the authority to lead the Special Force soldiers who will accompany you on this mission.

**Reuben:** Okay, sir.

**President:** Don’t disappoint me, Reuben.

---

### Scene 15.2
**Narrator:** The Special Force soldiers march to the Ashat land, striking against every man in the tribe. Bloodshed follows, and about a thousand men die before they retreat. Then the leader of the Ashat Tribe appears in the middle of the battlefield, challenging Reuben to a one-on-one duel.

---

### Scene 16.2
**Ashat Leader:** I don’t want more deaths on either side. Let’s do a duel. If you win, our tribe will surrender to you. But if you lose, you must retreat from this land.

**Reuben:** Alright. I’m not scared at all.

---

## Game 03 — Duel Against the Ashat Leader

### Scene 17.2
**Ashat Leader:** You are such a tough man. I acknowledge my loss. But I need to tell you something.

**Reuben:** About what?

**Ashat Leader:** The President is a very cruel leader. He wants to take our land, so we need to defend our tribe. I know that you are a good leader, so our tribe will support you if you want to rebel against the government.

**Choice:**
- Trust him
- Capture him

## Ending 1 — Revolution Ending

### Scene 17.2.1
**Narrator:** The rebellion marches toward the government palace. They clash with government soldiers, throwing the nation into chaos. Reuben enters the palace, searching for the government office to make sure the President has not escaped. But suddenly, someone stops him.

---

### Scene 18.2.1
**Commander:** Hahaha... so you finally found out the truth.

**Reuben:** Of course. I need to get to the government office. Move aside.

**Commander:** It’s too late. No one will save you now, and the Ashat Tribe will also perish.

**Reuben:** That will not happen.

**Commander:** Stop being so confident, Reuben. You won’t be able to defeat me.

---

## Game 04 — Duel Against the Commander

### Scene 19.2.1
**Narrator:** Reuben finally defeats the Commander and captures the President. Activists across the nation praise his bravery and tactics. He becomes the new ruler of the nation, bringing peace and justice to the citizens.

**Ending:** **Revolution Ending**

---

## Ending 2 — Become the Government

### Scene 17.2.2
**Narrator:** The Ashat Leader is executed by the government. Reuben becomes one of the greatest Special Forces in the nation, capturing anyone who attempts to rebel. Although some people see him as cruel, he continues to serve the government with absolute loyalty.

**Ending:** **Become the Government**
